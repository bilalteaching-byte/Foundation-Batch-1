Comparision Operators

  1. > , Greater then e.g . a > b
  2. >= , Greater then or Equals to e.g . a >= b
  3. < , less then e.g . a < b
  4. <= , less then or Equals to e.g . a <= b
  5. == , both values match e.g , a == b
  6. === , both values and there types should match e.g , a === b
  7. != , check if a not equals to b , e.g a != b
